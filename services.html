Our NDIS Services

SEMS provides a comprehensive range of NDIS services designed to support your independence, well-being, and community participation. Our person-centred approach ensures that each service is tailored to meet your unique needs and aspirations.

0107 Daily Personal Activities

Support with personal care activities including bathing, dressing, grooming, and other daily living tasks to promote dignity and independence.

0108 Assist Travel Transport

Transportation assistance to help you access community services, appointments, and social activities, promoting greater independence and community participation.

0115 Daily Tasks Shared Living

Support with daily tasks in shared living arrangements, helping you maintain your independence while living with others in a supportive environment.

0116 Innovative Community Participation

Creative and innovative programs designed to enhance your participation in community activities, fostering social connections and personal growth.

0117 Daily Living Life Skills

Skill development support to help you learn and maintain essential life skills for greater independence in your daily activities and routines.

0120 Household Tasks

Assistance with household management including cleaning, cooking, shopping, and home maintenance to help you live independently in your own space.

0125 Participate Community

Support to engage meaningfully with your local community, including recreational activities, social groups, and community events that align with your interests.

0136 Group Centre Based Activities

Structured group activities in centre-based settings that promote skill development, social interaction, and community connections in a supportive environment.

Our Service Approach

Person-Centred Focus

Every service is tailored to your individual needs, preferences, and goals, ensuring you remain at the centre of all decisions.

Skill Development

We focus on building your capabilities and independence through structured skill development and mentorship.

Community Integration

Our services actively promote genuine community participation, not just access, fostering meaningful connections.

Holistic Support

We coordinate with other providers and services to ensure you receive comprehensive, seamless support across all areas of your life.
